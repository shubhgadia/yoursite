About Us

About UnfollowerSpy

Built for people who value honesty on social media.

What we stand for

Clear answers, no Instagram login, no hidden storage, and no unnecessary friction. The product exists to help people understand their audience without giving up control of their data.

Our Mission

UnfollowerSpy exists to give people transparency over their Instagram following in a way that is private, simple, and free. Social media relationships are often opaque by design. We built a tool that gives users a direct answer without asking them to trust another service with their account credentials.

How It Works

1

Upload your data file

Download your Instagram export and upload the zip file directly into the tool.

2

We analyse it in your browser

The file is processed locally on your device rather than sent to our servers.

3

See who does not follow you back

The tool compares your followers and following lists and shows the difference clearly.

Why We Built This

Instagram used to make follower relationships feel simpler. Over time, the product moved away from giving people an easy, direct way to see who had unfollowed them or who never followed back in the first place. That gap created a wave of risky third-party apps asking for Instagram logins and broad permissions.

We did not think that tradeoff was acceptable. People should not have to hand over their account credentials just to get a basic answer about their own network. So we built a free, private alternative that requires no login and stores nothing.

Privacy First

Your Instagram data does not leave the browser while the audit runs. No Instagram login is required. No follower lists are stored on our servers. The product is intentionally built around local processing so you can use it without giving up control of your account data.
