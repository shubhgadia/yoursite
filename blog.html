Step by step guide to find your unfollowers

If you want a quick path, follow these exact steps. This process is simple, official, and works well whether you are using an iPhone, Android phone, or a laptop. It is one of the easiest ways to check who doesn't follow you back on Instagram without downloading a shady app.

  1. Open Instagram and go to your profile.
  2. Tap the three lines and go to Settings and privacy.
  3. Tap Account Centre at the bottom.
  4. Tap Your information and permissions.
  5. Tap Export your information then Create export.
  6. Set format to JSON and date range to All time.
  7. Select only Followers and Following in the customise section.
  8. Tap Create export and wait for the email from Instagram.
  9. Download the zip file from the email.
  10. Go to unfollowerspy.com and upload the zip file.
  11. Instantly see everyone who is not following you back.
