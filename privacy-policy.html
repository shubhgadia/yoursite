Privacy Policy

Your Instagram export stays on your device.

This page explains what UnfollowerSpy collects, what it does not collect, and how analytics and advertising tools may operate alongside the browser-based audit experience.

At a glance

UnfollowerSpy processes Instagram export files locally in your browser. We do not upload, store, or retain the contents of your Instagram export on our servers.

1. Overview

UnfollowerSpy is designed to analyze Instagram export files entirely in the browser. When you upload your Instagram zip file, the parsing and comparison work happens on your device. We do not store your Instagram export, follower data, usernames, messages, media, or any other Instagram content on our servers.

This local-processing approach is the foundation of the product. It reduces data exposure and keeps the analysis tied to your browser session rather than a remote database.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect the contents of your Instagram export. That means we do not intentionally receive or retain your followers list, following list, messages, photos, videos, stories, or profile data.

We may collect limited anonymous analytics information about how the website is used, such as page views, device type, browser type, general region, and interaction events. This information does not include the contents of your Instagram file.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to understand site performance, measure traffic, prevent abuse, and support advertising. These tools help us understand whether the site is functioning correctly and which pages are most useful.

Cookies used for analytics and advertising may be set by us or by third-party services integrated into the site.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

We may use Google AdSense or related Google advertising products to display ads on the site. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to show personalised or contextual ads based on your browsing activity and other signals.

You can learn more about how Google uses information in advertising and control ad personalisation through Google's settings: https://adssettings.google.com/.

5. Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site, such as which pages are viewed, how long sessions last, and what types of devices access the site. This information is used in aggregated form to improve usability and site reliability.

You can learn more about Google's practices and opt out using the official tools here: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

6. How We Use Information

Any limited analytics or advertising-related information may be used to operate the website, monitor performance, understand usage trends, improve the product, detect misuse, and support monetisation through advertising.

We do not use Instagram export contents for profiling, sale, sharing, or storage because those contents are not collected by the service.

7. Third-Party Links

This website may link to third-party services, including Instagram, Google, advertising settings pages, and analytics opt-out pages. Once you leave this site, those third parties control their own content and privacy practices.

You should review the privacy policies of any third-party services you visit through links on this site.

8. Children's Privacy

UnfollowerSpy is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the site, contact us so we can review the issue and take appropriate action.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights relating to personal information processed about you. These may include rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict certain data, and to withdraw consent where processing relies on consent.

  • Request access to information associated with you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing where consent is the basis used.

Because Instagram export contents are processed locally and not stored by us, many requests relating to uploaded Instagram data can be handled directly by closing the page or clearing browser data on your device.
